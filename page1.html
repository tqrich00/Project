Ready to dive into some of the things we have been learning this semester? Let's jump into some basic HTML!

I. Headings

i. Headings can be displayed in various levels from 1-6.

ii. Headings are block tags and they are used to open and close block elements.

II. Paragraphs

i. Paragraph tags are used to seperate blocks of text.

ii. Like headings, paragraphs are also block tags.

III. Semantic Tags

i. Semantic tags are utilized when structuring your webpage.

ii. They also assist individuals with screen readers by understanding which part of the page they are on.
